Building the Backbone of Connectivity

Gigatel Solutions owns and operates an intra-city underground fiber network of over 1000+ KMs, connecting Delhi NCR with Agra and Gwalior. Licensed with IP-1 from the Government of India, we have our optical fiber presence in dense parts of South Delhi, Noida and Gurgaon.

We provide last mile fiber connectivity to licensed Telecom Service Providers through our optical fiber network and a dynamic team of skilled professionals with expertise in Telecom infrastructure and services. We specialize in building and operating dark fiber networks using the latest technologies. Our existing Dark fiber network is built using ring topology, assuring protection and reliability to our client base. We undertake Turnkey OFC Projects, customized as per the client's requirements.
